## Theorem

If $T:\mathbf{R}^n\rightarrow\mathbf{R}^m$ is a linear function, then $T(\vec{0})=\vec{0}$.

<!--more-->

## Proof

$$\begin{align}
& T(\vec{0})=T(\vec{0}+\vec{0})\\
\Rightarrow&T(\vec{0})=T(\vec{0})+T(\vec{0})\\
\Rightarrow&T(\vec{0})=\vec{0}\qquad\square
\end{align}$$
